A Journey of Grace; A Night of Gratitude

Some people enter our lives and leave footprints upon our souls.

For the past three years, our parish has been a home—guided by the warmth, wisdom, and grace of our beloved Saju Chacko Achen and Family.

As they prepare to cast their nets into new waters, we invite all Yuvajanasakhyam members to gather for one final evening of vibrant fellowship.

Let us fill the air with music, the tables with laughter, and the night with stories that will never fade.

Distance is only a test of how far love can travel.

Together, we celebrate the chapters written and the many yet to come—moving toward new horizons with the same shared faith.

🔥 Let’s come together to set the night ablaze with memories that shaped us

As we honor and bid farewell to our beloved

Saju Achen and Family
